About The Devs.

All about your fabulous web developers, Seless Lautzenhiser, Soua (Pa) Xiong and Shelby Kelly.

Seless Lautzenhiser

Seless is a graduate of Johnson and Wales for Baking and Pastry Arts. She has 10 years of food service experience ranging from making burgers at Disneyland to creating beautiful pastry delicacies at a luxury retirement home. Seless has decided to take a step (or a leap, really) into web development with which she has very minimal experience. In school Seless experimented with different forms of art ranging from traditional pencil and paper to theater. However, she found her passion in graphic design and graphic communications. These years of experience in traditional art, design, and pastry arts has culminated in a very creatively inclined individual eager to grasp new concepts and learn a new trade. Her time in the UNCC Coding Bootcamp had been challenging but full of reward. She is one to constantly reach and seek out the next direction in her life, and this new direction is taking her to a whole new world of opportunity, and she could not be more excited.



Soua (Pa) Xiong

Pa graduated from Metropolitan State University, St. Paul, MN with her Biology and minor in Chemistry. She is currently working as a Clinical Research Coordinator conducting clinical drug trials. She has 2 kids and 3 dogs. She is always looking for new opportunity to grow her knowledge. Her favorite quote is “Knowledge has a beginning but no end.” Geeta Iyengar

Shelby Kelly

Shelby is a stay at home, homeschooling mother of three who dove in to web development as an extracurricular. She learned basic coding skills as a child, and always had a love for design and problem-solving. This made becoming a web dev a perfect fit. As an attendee of the University of North Carolina - Charlotte Coding Bootcamp, the program has expanded her skills exponentially. With an aim for front-end development, many of the user interface concepts displayed here are designed by Seless and herself.
